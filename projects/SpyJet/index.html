Do the spies of your LEGO city need to get to one place or another fast? Well this sweet ride can help! It has four stud shooters, three big jet, four wings and two for stability wing when going the speed of sound. This idea really came form the LEGO City Gallery on a project for there Afterburn Mission that I made. The model is made up of 159 parts, but there might be more brick added if where to become a set. Thanks for looking at my idea and hope you support!
I made this model in MecaBricks and rendered in Blender.
