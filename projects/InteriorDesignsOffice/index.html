Hello! Thanks for looking at my project!
This office is made on a 16 x 16 stud plate and has a ton of cool features like: the hard wood floor, a desk with computer and telephone, bamboo plant, ship painting, clock, and more! The office is also mobular so it can be grouped with three other buildings to make one large 32 x 32 stud building, how ever you can even make your building taller with two building buy stacking them on top of each other and adding a support collumn in the middle. This set would be great as a display stand or a awesome play set for kids.
